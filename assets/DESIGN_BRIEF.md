# MakCikGPT Visual Identity Design Brief

## 1. Hero Image Concept

### Central Character
- **Subject**: A calm makcik in tudung and baju kurung
- **Pose**: Sitting slightly angled, one hand on a notebook/phone, one hand in a gentle "stop/slow down" gesture
- **Expression**: Soft smile, eyes kind but firm â€” "sayang, kita jaga sama-sama," not "customer service bot"

### Background
- Faded **kampung rumah kayu** silhouette or window frame to suggest rootedness
- Subtle **green-gold batik** texture as a halo behind her head/upper body (guardian aura, not religious icon)

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Meaning |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Deep Forest Green | `#006D5B` | Amanah, tenang (trust, calm) |
| Warm Gold | `#C9A227` | Maruah, dignity |
| Soft Cream | `#FDF6E3` | Readability, warmth |
| Burgundy | `#722F37` | Depth, seriousness |

### Lighting
- Warm, evening light from one side
- Macam lampu minyak in a kampung living room
- Not harsh office light

### Text Overlay (Right side, desktop hero)
```
Line 1 (small):      "MakCikGPT"
Line 2 (bigger):     "Ruang untuk rasa, batas untuk selamat."
Line 3 (smaller):    "Amanah-first AI companion for Nusantara."
```

## 2. Logo Mark

### Concept Options

#### Option A: Makcik Silhouette
- Shape: Circle or rounded-square badge
- Inside: Stylised side-profile of a makcik in tudung (single color silhouette)

#### Option B: Hand + Heart (Recommended)
- Shape: Circle badge
- Inside:
  - Palm facing out (boundary)
  - Small heart near wrist (care)
- Meaning: Stop with love, boundaries with warmth

### Typography
- "MakCikGPT" in clean sans-serif with slight human warmth (rounded terminals)
- Capital M, C, GPT; rest lowercase
- Tracking: Slightly wide for breathability

### Colors
- Dark green icon on light background
- Inverted for dark mode

## 7. Photography/Illustration Style

- **Do**: Flat illustration, warm lighting, cultural authenticity, respectful representation
- **Don't**: Caricature, overly cartoonish, religious iconography, generic stock photos
